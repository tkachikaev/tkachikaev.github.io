BUBI · IN DEVELOPMENT

Bubi

A bright 2D platformer about a green bird that travels through game worlds, searches for secrets and learns to handle whatever lies ahead.

Forest world Unity 2D PC

ABOUT THE PROJECT

A game I want to play myself.

Bubi is taking shape as a warm and approachable adventure: not overly complex, but full of movement, exploration, hidden paths and worlds worth returning to. The forest is the foundation right now; ancient ruins, a desert and space levels will follow.

  • Clean cartoon 2D style with bright, easy-to-read worlds.
  • Levels with secrets, alternate paths and small discoveries.
  • New mechanics will unfold alongside Bubi’s journey.

GAMEPLAY FOUNDATION

Three things Bubi is built around.

The core mechanics already set the rhythm: easy to start, satisfying to move through, and fun to explore for the next path.

01

Movement and jumping

Responsive controls, a double jump and air control form the foundation for exploring each level.

02

Egg projectile

Bubi’s main ranged attack: it deals with enemies and leaves room for future gameplay ideas.

03

Worlds and secrets

Forest, ruins, desert and space will differ not only visually, but also in their pace and gameplay.

ENEMIES

The first enemies in Bubi’s world.

At first, the player will meet simple enemies, but their behavior and danger will grow over time.

REGULAR ENEMY

Slime

The simplest enemy in Bubi. It slowly moves along the ground, blocks the path, and makes the player jump or attack at the right moment.

  • TypeGround
  • SpeedSlow
  • ThreatBasic
